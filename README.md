## Final exam pack

- `00_final_exam_scope.html` validates the final exam screenshot against the existing pages and flags extra sections that are present in the notes but not in the screenshot scope.
- `08_machine_learning_knn.html` covers ML basics and K-nearest neighbour.
- `09_ann_deep_learning.html` covers ANN properties and deep learning.
- `10_backpropagation.html` covers feedforward learning, backpropagation, optimisers, and batch terminology.
- `11_hopfield_networks.html` covers Hopfield training and application calculations.
- `12_kmeans_clustering.html` covers K-means clustering and the worked centroid example.

## What this pack now focuses on

- The sections match the uploaded test-scope image exactly.
- Practical content is prioritised over long theory passages.
- Algorithms are written in execution order where possible.
- Parameters and equations are called out explicitly.
- Worked patterns are included for selection methods, ACO, and GE mapping.
